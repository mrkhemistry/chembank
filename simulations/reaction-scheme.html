How to use

Each question gives a starting material and a target product. Click reagents on the right to transform the current structure. Reach the target in the suggested number of steps.

Hints

Click Hint to highlight a valid next reagent on the shortest path. Use Undo to step back, Reset to return to the starting material, or click any structure in your route to branch from there.
